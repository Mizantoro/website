Declaration of Values

By using mizantoro.net you agree to the following values:

  • You condemn Russia's unlawful aggression against the sovereign state of Ukraine.
  • You support Ukraine's sovereignty over territories under Russian occupation.
  • You condemn Russia's hybrid warfare targeting democratic states, with particular emphasis on Finland, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, and Poland.
  • You recognize Russia as a terrorist state.
  • You recognize and respect international law.
  • You respect the human rights of all people, regardless of race, sex, nationality, or age.
  • You recognize that every nation has the right to be represented by a democratic government operating within its territory.
  • You recognize Hamas as a terrorist organization responsible for attacks on civilians.
  • You recognize that Israel's military actions in Gaza causing civilian casualties are unlawful and constitute crimes against humanity.
  • You reject any ideologies that promote hatred, violence, or oppression, including but not limited to communism, Nazism, fascism, racism, and accelerationist movements. You oppose movements or beliefs that seek to undermine human dignity, force violent revolutions, or discriminate against individuals based on race, gender, religion, nationality, or any other characteristic.
  • You recognize and condemn Chinese aggression against the sovereign state of Taiwan.
  • You condemn Chinese war crimes against minorities, including the Uyghurs.
